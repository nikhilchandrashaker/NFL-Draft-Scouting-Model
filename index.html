Draft Steals & Reaches

Comparing actual draft slot to what a combine-measurables-only model expected. Steal = fell later than his workout numbers suggested. Reach = drafted earlier than workout numbers alone would predict. Read the caveat below before treating this as a scouting verdict.

Caveat: the underlying model only explains ~20% of draft-position variance (R² ≈ 0.21) — tape, production, and intangibles matter far more than workout numbers, and this model sees none of that. Because predictions regress toward the mean, top-10 picks will systematically look like "reaches" and Day 3 picks will look like "steals." Treat this as "what the model can't explain," not a scouting grade.
